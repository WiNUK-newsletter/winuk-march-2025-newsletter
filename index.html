IDWGS:

The International Day of Women and Girls in Science is celebrated on February 11th each year. It's all about recognising the achievements of women and girls in science, highlighting their contributions, and promoting gender equality in STEM fields (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics).
It matters because historically, women have been underrepresented in these fields despite having made significant contributions. This day raises awareness about the importance of empowering women and girls to pursue careers in science and technology, ensuring they have equal opportunities for education, research, and professional growth. By encouraging more women and girls to enter STEM, we can tap into a broader talent pool, foster innovation, and address global challenges more effectively.

WiNUK aims to empower women in neuroscience by amplifying voices, fostering connections, and creating opportunities for growth. Together, we‚Äôre breaking barriers and inspiring the next generation of female neuroscientists!
Make sure to check our social media platforms to see all the inspiring posts about women who continue to inspire and excel in neuroscience and beyond.
